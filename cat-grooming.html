Cat Grooming

Gentle, patient grooming for cats too.

Book this service Call us

What it is

Cats are welcome here. Owner Dawnell has groomed cats for over a decade. We offer gentle baths, brush outs, tidy trims, and lion cuts for cats that are matting or shedding heavily, all at a calm, unhurried pace.

What's included

  • Gentle bath and dry
  • Brush out or lion cut
  • Nail trim
  • Ear cleaning

Good for

Cats that are matting, shedding a lot, or need a fresh lion cut.

How long it takes

About 1 to 2 hours.

Pricing

Pricing depends on breed, size, and coat. Call or book online for a quick quote.
